PCA¶

Standardization data for PCA¶

In PCA, we should standardize the variables before applying PCA because it will give more emphasis to those variables having higher variances than to those variables with very low variances while identifying the right principle component.

Also standardizing is important in PCA since it is a variance maximizing technique. It projects your original data onto directions which maximize the variance.

By doing so, all the features contribute to the variance explanation.

Most of the distribution of columns follow normal distribution. Hence we can scale them using Standard Scaler to preprocess the data.

Standard Scaler standardize features by removing the mean and scaling to unit variance.
Each value is reduced using the following formula $$ z = \frac {x - \mu}{\sigma} $$ Where x is the value,
$\mu$ is the mean of that columns,
$\sigma$ is the standard deviation of that columns.
